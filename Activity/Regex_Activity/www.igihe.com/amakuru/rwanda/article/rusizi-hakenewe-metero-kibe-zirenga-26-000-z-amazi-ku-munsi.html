Rusizi: Hakenewe metero kibe zirenga 26.000 z’amazi ku munsi

Yanditswe na Iradukunda Serge
Kuya 25 November 2025 saa 12:51
Yasuwe :
Yavuzweho:

Ubuyobozi bw’Akarere ka Rusizi bwatangaje ko abaturage b’aka karere ku munsi bakenera metero kibe 26.245 z’amazi ariko inganda zihari zikaba zifite ubushobozi bwo gutunganya metero kibe 10.365.

Byagaragarijwe Abadepite bari mu Karere ka Rusizi muri gahunda yo kumenya no kugenzura ibikorwa bya Guverinoma, aho by’umwihariko bari kureba ibijyanye n’iterambere ry’imijyi, imikoreshereze y’ubutaka, bakanareba imyubakire n’imiturire.

Abadepite bari gusura uturere kuva tariki 23-29 Ugushyingo 2025. Ni isura ribaye mu gihe mu Karere ka Rusizi hari imiyoboro 54 y’amazi ifite uburebure bw’ibilometero 990,79.

Iyo miyoboro ku munsi inyuramo amazi angana na m3 10.365, mu gihe abaturage bakenera m3 26 245, bivuze ko bafite icyuho cya m3 15.880. Ni mu gihe mu 2035, abatuye Akarere ka Rusizi bazaba bakenera 31.977 ku munsi.

Mu Karere ka Rusizi haracyagaragara ibura ry’amazi rya hato na hato, ubuyobozi buvuga ko riterwa n’isaranganya kuko hakiri icyuho hagati y’amazi atunganywa n’ayo abaturage bakeneye.

Umuyobozi w’ishami ry’ubutaka n’imiturire mu karere ka Rusizi, Hagenimana Godefroid, yasabye abadepite ko bakorera aka karere ubuvugizi kugira ngo haboneke ingengo y’imari yo kongera ingano y’amazi kuko nta gikozwe ikibazo cy’ubuke bw’amazi cyazaba ingorabahizi mu myaka iri imbere bigendanye n’uko abaturage bazagenda biyongera.

Ati “Uyu munsi amazi dutunganya ni make cyane dukeneye menshi, ni ikibazo gikomeye cyazanakomeza kwiyongera mu gihe haba nta gikozwe kuko mu 2035 abatuye Akarere ka Rusizi bazaba bakenera m3 31.000 z’amazi ku munsi”

Umujyi wa Rusizi wonyine ukenera m3 9000 ku munsi, ugahabwa m3 4000 bivuze ko wonyine ufite icyuho cya m3 5000.

Hagenimana avuga ko ubuke bw’amazi mu mujyi wa Rusizi ari imbogamizi ku isuku n’isukura, akavuga ko hakenewe ingengo y’imari yo gutunganya isoko ya Gisuma mu Murenge wa Nyakarenzo.

Ati “Iyo soko itunganyijwe yaduha m3 3000 ku munsi, mu mujyi wa Rusizi tukagira m3 7000 tugasigarana icyuho cya m3 2000 ku munsi”.

Mbaringayaho Ali, utuye mu mujyi wa Rusizi yavuze ko abatuye uyu mujyi babangamiwe n’ibura ry’amazi rya hato na hato asaba Leta ko yabafasha kubona ibigega by’amazi ku giciro gito.

Depite Nabahire Anastase, uyoboye itsinda ry’Abadepite basuye Akarere ka Rusizi, yashimye igitekerezo cy’uko abaturage bafashwa kubona ibigega, agaragaza ko abaturage ibisubizo byose, badakwiye kubyitega kuri Leta, ahubwo abagira inama yo kwishyira hamwe, bakareba umucuruzi ubazanira ibyo bigega bakazajya bamwishyura gahoro gahoro.

Ati “Bizanadufasha guhangana na cya kibazo nababwiraga cy’amazi y’imvura agenda adutwariye ubutaka kandi tubukeneye. Ubutaka tugomba kubugumana tukabugaburira kugira ngo nabwo butugaburire”.

Inganda z'i Rusizi zitunganya metero kibe 10.000 z'amazi mu gihe hakenewe metero kibe 26.000
